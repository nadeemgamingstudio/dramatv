Privacy Policy Overview

Welcome to Drama TV! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe, transparent streaming experience.

Important Notice: Drama TV is designed with your privacy first. We do not collect any personal information. All your watch history, likes, and comments are stored locally on your device only.

By using Drama TV, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. We may update this policy from time to time, and any changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.

Information Collection

Drama TV collects minimal information necessary for basic functionality:

  • Watch History & Preferences: Your viewed videos, likes, and comments are saved locally on your device using your browser’s local storage. This data never leaves your phone.
  • Anonymous Usage Statistics: Completely anonymized, non‑identifiable data to help us improve app performance (e.g., crash reports, feature popularity).
  • Device Information: Basic device model and OS version for compatibility optimization – collected anonymously.
We DO NOT Collect: Names, email addresses, phone numbers, contacts, location, payment information, or any sensitive personal data. No login or account is required.

All video streaming is performed directly from our CDN partners (Hugging Face). No personal data is transmitted during video playback.

Third-Party Services

Drama TV uses the following third-party services to deliver its features:

  • Google Admob: For displaying advertisements. Admob may collect device information, advertising ID (resettable by user), IP address (anonymized), and app usage data. Learn more.
  • Hugging Face CDN: We stream video content from Hugging Face’s publicly available datasets. No personal information is shared with this service; standard CDN logs may include anonymized IP addresses.
Your Control: You can reset your advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads at any time through your device settings.

We carefully select partners who comply with privacy regulations. However, we are not responsible for the independent privacy practices of these third parties.

Data Security

We take data security seriously and have implemented the following measures:

  • Local Storage Only: All watch history, likes, and comments stay exclusively on your device inside your browser’s local storage. We have no servers that store your activity.
  • No Transmission: The app never sends your personal data to any remote server.
  • No Accounts: Because we don’t collect any personal information, there is no risk of account breaches.
  • Secure Ad Delivery: Ads are served through Google’s secure infrastructure, which follows industry‑standard security practices.
  • Regular Updates: We continuously review and update our dependencies to fix any potential vulnerabilities.
Security Note: While no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, we believe local‑only storage provides the highest level of privacy for our users.

Children's Privacy

Drama TV is suitable for users of all ages and contains no age‑restricted content:

  • No personal information is collected from children or adults.
  • All video content is family‑friendly drama shorts.
  • Ads shown are based on device‑level advertising ID; parents may restrict personalized ads via device settings.
  • We encourage parents to supervise children’s app usage and discuss responsible online behaviour.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through our app (which we do not collect), please contact us immediately so we can investigate.

For Parents: Drama TV does not require any account creation, and there is no way for a child to submit personal data through the app.

Your Rights & Controls

As a Drama TV user, you have full control over your data:

  • View History: Your watch history is stored locally – you can clear it anytime by clearing the app’s data or simply uninstalling.
  • Likes & Comments: Stored only on your device. Uninstalling the app removes them permanently.
  • Advertising Preferences: You can reset your advertising ID or opt out of personalised ads through your device’s “Ads” or “Privacy” settings.
  • Complete Deletion: Since we don’t keep any cloud data, uninstalling Drama TV instantly removes all locally stored information.
  • Access Requests: Because we hold no personal data, there is nothing to access or export – it’s all on your device.
Data Deletion: To remove all Drama TV data from your device, simply uninstall the app. No further action is required.
